ABOUT THE GAME
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Chees is a unique twist on classic chess where the rules change with every 
match! Each game begins with random "mutations" that transform how pieces move 
- a Pawn might act like a Rook, a Knight could move like a Pawn, or a Bishop 
might gain the power of a Queen.

This innovative mechanic creates endless strategic possibilities, turning each 
match into a fresh puzzle that challenges even experienced chess players.

KEY FEATURES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
• MUTATION SYSTEM: Every match features randomized piece behavior changes
• CLASSIC PIXEL ART: Beautiful hand-crafted pixel art graphics
• COZY ATMOSPHERE: Relaxing tavern setting with warm, inviting visuals
• ONLINE MULTIPLAYER: Compete against players worldwide with MMR-based matchmaking
• LOCAL PLAY: Play against friends or challenge the AI
• MULTIPLE LANGUAGES: Full localization in 5 languages
